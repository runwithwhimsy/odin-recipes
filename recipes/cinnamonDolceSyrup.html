Cinnamon Dolce Syrup

This syrup is great for adding warmth to your cafe latte or tea latte all year round.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cinnamon sticks, crushed

Steps

  1. Bring water and sugars to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat, stirring often.
  2. Reduce syrup to a simmer. Add vanilla and broken cinnamon sticks. Continue to stir (especially edges and bottom of pan) and simmer for 10 minutes. Syrup will begin to thicken.
  3. Carefully pour the syrup and cinnamon sticks into a heat-proof container (like a Pyrex measuring cup) and allow the syrup to cool and thicken for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Pour the syrup through a strainer to catch the cinnamon stick pieces into a bottle.
  5. Store cinnamon dolce syrup in the refrigerator.
  6. To use, stir 2 teaspoons (more or less) into a cup of coffee or tea.
