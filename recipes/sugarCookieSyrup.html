Sugar Cookie Syrup

This holiday syrup is perfect in your cup of coffee because, for some reason, it is unacceptable to eat cookies all day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon butter extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract

Steps

  1. Put water and sugar in a medium saucepan and bring mixture to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, lower heat and let simmer for about 10-15 minutes, until sugar is completely dissolved and mixture has thickened a bit.
  3. Remove saucepan from heat and add vanilla, butter, and almond extracts and stir.
  4. Pour syrup into a mason jar or bowl and place in fridge to cool completely before using. Syrup will continue to thicken as it cools.
